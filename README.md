💧 Water Drop Catcher — charity: water

- A browser-based arcade game built to raise awareness about the global water crisis. Catch clean water drops to fill a village well, dodge contaminated water, and learn real facts about water access around the world.

🎮 How to Play
- Move your bucket left and right to catch falling water drops
- Catch blue drops (💧) — clean water that fills the well
- Avoid brown drops (🟤) — contaminated water that costs you a life
- Fill the well to 100% to win
- You have 3 lives — lose them all and the game ends

📖 Features
- Fact popups — the game pauses at score milestones to share real statistics about the global water crisis
- Progressive difficulty — drops fall faster and spawn more frequently as you play
- Well progress bar — tracks how close you are to filling the well
- Sound effects — audio feedback for clean catches, dirty hits, and winning
- Responsive design — playable on desktop and mobile

🌍 The Cause
- This game was built in support of charity: water, a nonprofit bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in developing countries.

- 785 million people — 1 in 10 — lack access to clean water
- Women and girls spend 200 million hours every day collecting water
charity: water directs 100% of public donations to water projects


🛠️ Built With

- Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks or libraries
- Google Fonts (Playfair Display, DM Sans)
- SVG for the bucket catcher graphic
- CSS animations for drops, stars, and UI effects
